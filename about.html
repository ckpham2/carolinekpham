Hi, I'm Caroline!

I have backgrounds in visual design & business marketing

I studied business marketing in undergrad and took some classes in graphic design. I have honed my visual design skills through my job as a Marketing Administrator as well as on my free time.

As a creative soul and UX/UI Designer, I find deep meaning and passion in the art of storytelling and collaboration. Being well-versed in branding, visual and interaction design, I strive to bridge intuitive design & interactions with seamless user experiences to help elevate users' needs and goals through memorable, minimal interfaces. I believe that understanding the heart and vision of an idea while keeping in mind the users' needs throughout the entire design process is vital to creating unique and happy experiences for all users.
